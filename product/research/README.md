# Research

This folder is for understanding the case partner and building the product strategy around their needs.

---

## Structure

| File | Purpose |
|------|---------|
| `CASE-BRIEF.md` | The challenge as given — verbatim or summarized |
| `PARTNER-ANALYSIS.md` | Who is the case partner, their industry, pain points, landscape |
| `MARKET-FIT.md` | Target user, value proposition, competitive positioning |
| `BUSINESS-CASE.md` | Why this solution makes business sense — ROI, cost savings, scalability |
| `VALUE-ROADMAP.md` | MVP → 6 month → 12 month vision |

---

## Principle

We are not building a cool demo. We are building a product that solves a real problem for the case partner. The tech is the enabler, not the story. The story is: we understand your problem, here's a solution that fits your market, and here's why it scales.
